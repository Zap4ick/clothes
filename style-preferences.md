# My Style Preferences

## General vibe

- I prefer a minimalist, clean aesthetic
- Casual but put-together looks over formal wear
- Comfort is important — I avoid anything too tight or restrictive

## Colors & patterns

- I lean toward neutral and earth tones: black, white, grey, navy, beige, olive
- I rarely wear loud patterns or bright colors
- I like monochromatic or tonal outfits

## Occasions

- Most of my days are casual (work from home or relaxed office)
- For evenings out I prefer smart-casual, not suits
- I dislike overly sporty looks outside of actual sport

## Things to avoid

- Suits and ties unless absolutely necessary
- Graphic tees with large logos
- Overly baggy silhouettes
