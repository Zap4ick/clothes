# Style Preferences Template

Use this file as a starting point, then copy it to `style-preferences.md` and customize the copy with your own preferences.

## About You

Add any details that help the assistant make better outfit suggestions, such as age range, height, fit preferences, or daily context.

## Aesthetic Direction

Describe the overall style you like in plain language. Include the kinds of looks, brands, or references that fit you.

## Color Palette

List the colors, tones, and contrasts you tend to wear or avoid.

## Fit and Silhouette

Note whether you prefer slim, relaxed, structured, oversized, layered, or otherwise specific proportions.

## Rules and Constraints

Call out anything the assistant should avoid, such as loud logos, certain fabrics, or dress codes.

## Extra Notes

Add any other preferences, pain points, or context that should influence outfit suggestions.
